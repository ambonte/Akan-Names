Akan names

This web application takes user's birhday and calculates the day they were born and finally output their Akan's name according their gender.Akan names aree derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.
